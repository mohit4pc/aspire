    About CLC

    CLC is a leading educational institute in India that provides comprehensive test Preparatory services to students preparing for medical and engineering entrance exams, school/board exams Olympiads and NDA. CLC was established in 1996 in Sikar (Raj.) by a visionary young Engineer Sharwan Choudhary.

    CLC is the first coaching institute of Sikar. It was the zeal of team CLC that today Sikar is known as education city & students from all over India come here to fulfill their dreams. In the coaching field CLC presents lots of innovations timely to enlighten the result …to boost up the students to touch the sky. Every innovation aims only student’s well-fair in salutary result. Some of the innovations are:- Daily test series, Bio-Chem Test, Physics Echo ,Daily Assembly, Night Camps. Today these leading innovations of ours are followed by others and that is the self-authentication.

    First of all CLC believes in commitment, we provide the best systematic academic programme to our students at the time of admission .CLC has the best faculties with vast knowledge & superb experience which will help to each student for success in NEET & JEE. We believe in quality education so we provides every modern method of teaching and a competitive environment that aims to help our students to achieve their aim of life i.e. CLC teaches how to live the life.. and how to win…

    We provide personalized attention to each of our students to boost up the performance round the session. In CLC there is direct connection between Director & students. Each and Every Student meet with director for motivation and to get solution of any of the problem. CLC on toes for student round the clock.

    CLC is one and only coaching of Sikar which performs the best In NEET as well as JEE & different Olympiads.

    In the time of COVID, almost all of the students are too much disappointed because many of the students can’t complete the syllabus and many of the students feel depressed that how they revise the syllabus, how their doubts will be cleared, meanwhile CLC presents its YouTube channel open for all and CLC Online Classes to help every student to achieve their set target. At CLC YouTube channel we provide Guruneeti (previous year papers of NEET and JEE with solution) and almost 100 plus tests available for all (CLCian and Non-CLCians too) to revise the syllabus as well as free doubt removal session. In all scenario Students are the First. We do all everything possible to boost up the performance of our students at the pinnacle.

    Today CLC is one of the best educational groups of India for NEET, JEE, OLYMPIADS, School education (CBSE and RBSE) and NDA Academy. The trust of more than 1.30 Lakh Parents and students empower us… Today thousands of Doctors and Engineers from courtyard of Rajasthan is the story of CLC & this is the educational revolution.
